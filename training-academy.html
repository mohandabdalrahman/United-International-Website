TRAINING ACADEMY

We believe that, improvement in productivity, quality, cost, and time is critical in any organization and training is a key player in achieving these goals .

Thus, UIA provides:

  • Training solutions for all organizational levels, from entry-level to advanced specialist training.
  • Customized training that suites client and industry requirements.
  • Online, classroom and blended forms of workshops
  • Varieties of ‘off the shelf’ generally demanded training

Benefits of delivering training at your Organization

  • Cost and time saving options to suit your budget.
  • Relevant training for your staff and tailored to your organizational requirements; your organizational documentation, case studies typical workplace scenarios can be included.
  • Unique materials with your company identity with branding and images.
  • Convenient training options to suit your employees; choose the best: Learning methods: classroom or online learning or a mixture of the two Timescales, dates and location
  • If you have staff that needs to complete an in-house training, we can come to your organization in any part of the country to train your staff or alternatively provide flexible online e-learning.
  • We can also develop relevant content with any organizational specifics you require

Digital learning

  • Digital / Virtual Learning is synchronous live lessons broadcast over the web and organized in a live virtual meeting room where the learning session can be conducted using audio, video and whiteboards to simulate the natural environment.
  • Live online classes require trainees to be online at the same time as the education or training session.
  • Main features and benefits: A reliable, comfortable and safe learning environment Flexible learning about location Cost-effective, time-efficient, scalable and easily expandable
  • Online meetings and training using the “Zoom” application:
  • With the meeting apps such as Zoom, events will be informative, interactive and fun.
  • The app also allows learners to join live training sessions wherever they are, thus giving them more flexibility in terms of attending their sessions.

The main benefits of those applications for meetings and training include the following:

  • High quality video and audio Built-in collaboration tools.
  • Multiple participants can share their screens at once, and share in comments for a more interactive meeting.
  • Secure Encounter: Encryption, Role-Based Security, Password Protection, Attendance On Hold.
  • Team chat: group chat, searchable history, integrated file sharing, archive.
  • Sub rooms.
  • Share content, videos, or music.
  • Whiteboard feature for collaborative activities.
  • Mobile support: turn on default wallpapers and select an image of your choice, share your screen and comments.

Blended learning:

  • Blended learning describes a hybrid model of e-learning that allows the presence of traditional teaching methods alongside modern e-learning resources and activities in a single course.
  • The choice isn’t just between digital or face to face, but to design the perfect blend for your learners, using whichever tools are best for the job.
  • Enhancing student performance by employing technological innovations.
  • Increase direct and indirect interaction with teachers and with educational content.
  • It provides flexibility in the time of learning and the time of enrollment in its programs.
  • Taking into account the individual differences between learners so that each learner can proceed in learning according to his needs and abilities.
